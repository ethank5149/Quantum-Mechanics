# Heatsink Potential

For $n\in\{x\:|\:x=2n+1\:\forall\:n\in \Z\}$:

$$
V_n(x) = \left\{
\begin{array}{cc}
      V_0, & 0 \leq x \leq L/n \\
      0, & L/n < x \leq 2L/n \\
      V_0, & 2L/n < x \leq 3L/n \\
      \vdots&\vdots\\
      V_0, & \frac{n-3}{n}L \leq x \leq \frac{n-2}{n}L \\
      0, & \frac{n-2}{n}L < x \leq \frac{n-1}{n}L \\
      V_0, & \frac{n-1}{n}L < x \leq L \\
\end{array}
    \right.
$$
